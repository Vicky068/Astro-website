Vedic Planetary Remedies

Sun (Surya)

Practice: Offer water to rising Sun (Arghya), respect father.

Mantra: "Om Suryaya Namaha" (108 times at sunrise).

Moon (Chandra)

Practice: Drink from silver cup, avoid wasting water, respect mother.

Mantra: "Om Chandraya Namaha" (108 times at evening).

Mars (Mangal)

Practice: Donate red lentils on Tuesdays, do physical activity.

Mantra: "Om Angarakaya Namaha" (108 times on Tuesdays).

Mercury (Budha)

Practice: Feed green grass to cows, cultivate leafy plants.

Mantra: "Om Budhaya Namaha" (108 times on Wednesdays).

Jupiter (Guru)

Practice: Respect gurus/teachers, donate yellow foods on Thursdays.

Mantra: "Om Gurave Namaha" (108 times).

Venus (Shukra)

Practice: Cleanliness, wear white clothes, respect partners.

Mantra: "Om Shukraya Namaha" (108 times on Fridays).

Saturn (Shani)

Practice: Help laborers, servants, avoid lying, light oil lamp on Saturdays.

Mantra: "Om Sham Shanishcharaya Namaha" (108 times).
