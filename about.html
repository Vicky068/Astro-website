About Me

My journey through the stars and beyond

Meet Siddharth

Siddharth, widely recognized for his myth-free and prediction-oriented approach to astrology, is also an Accountant and Auditor based in Australia.

My journey into astrology began over 15 years ago when I was seeking answers to life's deeper questions. What started as curiosity quickly evolved into a passion as I discovered the profound insights that astrological analysis could provide.

Unlike conventional astrologers, I approach this ancient science with a modern, analytical mindset. My background in accounting and auditing has equipped me with a methodical approach to pattern recognition and data interpretation – skills that I apply to my astrological practice.

My Philosophy

Myth-Free Approach

I believe in separating astrological science from superstition. My readings focus on planetary influences backed by pattern observation, not fear-based predictions or rigid dogma.

Guidance, Not Destiny

The stars influence but do not dictate. I view astrology as a tool for self-awareness and decision-making, not as a deterministic force that removes your free will.

Balanced Perspective

Every astrological aspect has both challenges and opportunities. I provide a balanced view that acknowledges difficulties while highlighting potential growth areas.

Beyond Astrology
