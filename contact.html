Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a consultation?

You can book a consultation through our online booking system on the "Book Online" page. Select your preferred service, date, and time, fill in your details, and complete the payment process. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the necessary information.

What information do you need for an accurate reading?

For the most accurate reading, we need your date of birth, time of birth (as precise as possible), and place of birth. If you don't know your exact birth time, please mention this when booking, and we can discuss alternative approaches during your consultation.

How are consultations conducted?

All consultations are conducted online via video call (Zoom, Google Meet, or WhatsApp) or phone call, based on your preference. You'll receive connection details after booking. We serve clients worldwide, so we can accommodate different time zones.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment is processed securely at the time of booking, and you'll receive a receipt via email.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time at no additional cost. Please use the rescheduling link in your confirmation email or contact us directly for assistance.
